About VidFetch

🎯 Mission

VidFetch provides a simple, privacy-respecting way to download videos from popular platforms without requiring any local software or FFmpeg. Our server uses pre-merged formats only — no merging, no conversion, just direct downloads.

🔒 Privacy

We do not log your downloads, store any files permanently, or track behavior. All downloaded videos are automatically deleted from our server after you receive them. No third-party analytics.

⚖️ Legal

VidFetch is a tool for downloading videos you have permission to save. Respect copyright laws and platform terms of service. We are not responsible for misuse.

Supported Platforms (1000+)

  • YouTube (1080p/720p/480p combined)
  • TikTok (no watermark option)
  • Facebook Watch
  • Twitter/X videos
  • Vimeo
  • Twitch Clips
  • Dailymotion
  • Bilibili, Reddit, Rumble, and many more

* Instagram, Netflix, and DRM-protected sites are not supported due to login requirements.

Tech Stack

  • Backend: Flask (Python 3)
  • Extraction: yt-dlp (no FFmpeg, combined formats only)
  • Frontend: Vanilla JS, CSS3, HTML5
  • Real-time: Server-Sent Events (SSE)
  • Storage: In-memory jobs + temp files

Statistics

⚡ Average download speed: up to 50 MB/s

📁 Max file size: 4GB (temporary)

🌐 Global CDN delivery

🕒 Auto-cleanup every download

VidFetch is powered by yt-dlp and designed to respect platform limitations. Always follow fair usage policies.
